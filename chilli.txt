Ingredients:
 - 500g mince meat
 - herbs
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 celerey sticks
 - 2 chillis
 - 1 tin kidney beans
 - 1 litre of beef stock
 - 1 tin butter beans
 - 1 tin chopped tomatoes

Directions:
 1. Fry the mince in medium heat until brown
 
 2. Add the chillis, spices/herbs, kidney beans and butter beans and mix in.
 
 3. Add the stock and the rest of the ingredients then simmer for about 1 hour
